Our Adventures

For us, adventures mean pushing boundaries, discovering new places, and making unforgettable memories. We offer exciting rafting experiences on some of the most thrilling rivers, where adventure awaits around every bend. Join us and be part of the adventure!

Possibilities

Río Usumacinta: The Usumacinta River is known for its powerful currents and stunning jungle landscapes. It flows through Mexico and Guatemala, offering thrilling rapids and incredible wildlife sightings. Rafting Price: $120 usd per person

 Book Now

Río Grijalva: A scenic river that winds through lush forests and impressive canyons. It’s perfect for both beginners and experienced rafters, with moderate rapids and breathtaking views. Rafting Price: $100 usd per person

 Book Now

Río Tampaón: The Tampaón River features fast-moving waters and exhilarating rapids, nestled in the heart of the Huasteca region. It is ideal for adrenaline seekers who love adventure. Rafting Price: $150 usd per person

 Book Now

Río Palizada: With calmer currents and pristine surroundings, Río Palizada offers a more relaxed rafting experience, perfect for families and nature lovers who wish to enjoy the tranquility of the river. Rafting Price: $90 usd per person

 Book Now

Río Micos Famous for its crystal-clear waters and stunning waterfalls, Río Micos provides a mix of fun rapids and serene stretches. It’s an ideal destination for a fun-filled day on the water. Rafting Price: $110 usd per person
